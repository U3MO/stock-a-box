# Stock-A-Box — Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 2026  
**Last updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

Stock-A-Box stores the following data to operate the virtual market:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Your Discord User ID (a numeric identifier assigned by Discord) | Associates in-game data with your account |
| Cash balance, share portfolio, open orders, loans, debts | Core game state |
| Transaction history | Portfolio tracking and admin oversight |
| IPO applications (ticker, name, sector, description, starting price) | Processing your listing request |
| DM subscription preferences | Sending you opted-in stock updates |

We do **not** collect your Discord username, email address, password, payment details, or any other personal information beyond what is listed above.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used **exclusively** to:

- Display your portfolio, balance, and transaction history within the Bot.
- Process in-game trades, loans, dividends, limit orders, and IPO applications.
- Send you DM updates for stocks you have subscribed to.
- Allow administrators to manage the in-game economy (e.g. correcting data after an exploit).

**Your data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.**

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- All data is stored in JSON files on the server(s) hosting the Bot.
- **Hourly backups** are retained for up to **5 days**, after which they are automatically deleted.
- Your data is only accessible to the bot administrator(s).
- Reasonable precautions are taken to secure the hosting server, but no system is perfectly secure. Use of the Bot is at your own risk.

---

## 4. Your Right to Data Removal

> You have the right to request **permanent deletion** of all data associated with your Discord User ID at any time.

A full data removal ("wipe") includes:

- Your portfolio and cash balance
- All open orders, loans, and debts
- Your full transaction history — both live data and **all backup copies**
- Any IPO applications you have submitted
- Your DM subscription preferences

### How to request removal

Contact a bot administrator and ask for a **"data wipe"** for your account. Administrators will remove your data from the live database and all existing backup snapshots.

> **Note:** Stocks you have listed as an IPO owner will not be automatically removed, as they affect other users' portfolios. The administrator will discuss options with you in that case.

Requests are typically processed within **48 hours**.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Your in-game data is retained for as long as you actively use the Bot, or until you request deletion. Backup snapshots are automatically pruned after **5 days**. If the Bot is decommissioned or the hosting server is shut down, all data will be deleted.

---

- [Discord Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy)

The Bot operator has no control over Discord's data practices.

---

## 7. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your right to data deletion, or want to report a security concern, please contact a bot administrator directly via Discord.
